What We Do

Specialist Residential Care for Young People

Our Witham Home

Comfort Ark Haven provides a welcoming, family-style residential home in Witham, carefully situated near local amenities, schools, and community resources. Our home offers a safe, nurturing environment with easy access to activities, transport links, and educational opportunities.

Referral Process

We maintain a clear and structured referral process for planned placements. Emergency admissions are considered on a case-by-case basis where appropriate. Our team works closely with referring agencies to ensure smooth transitions.

Our Residential Home

Comfort Ark Haven provides a family-style residential home in Witham, Essex. Our home is thoughtfully located near local amenities, activities, public transport, and learning opportunities, ensuring young people have everything they need to thrive.

In our home, young people benefit from:

  • Support with life skills development and transition planning
  • A warm, welcoming living space designed for comfort and security
  • A highly trained and experienced staff team
  • Access to therapeutic and counseling services when needed
  • Individualized support with favorable staff-to-resident ratios
  • Focus on building meaningful, trusting relationships
  • Regular workshops covering self-care, independence, and social skills
  • Educational support including tutoring when required
  • Cultural awareness activities promoting inclusivity and respect
  • Engaging activities, trips, and community experiences
  • Connections to local support services and healthcare resources
